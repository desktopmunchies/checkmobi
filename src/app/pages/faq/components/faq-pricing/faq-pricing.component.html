Pricing

Why do I need to pay subscription if I only need SMS verification?
We ask for subscription because:
  1. We have additional platform features for all our products including SMS such as: Multiple carrier support where you can route SMS traffic between two very good carriers. They can also be used in round robin for higher chance of delivering the SMS. By suing CheckMobi you will get all our upcoming platform updates and features.
  2. Our low SMS rates. We don’t have special plans based on volume, the lowest rate we can use we give it to all startups. We have to cover our 24/7 support cost through a monthly fee. There are services that don’t charge for subscription but they are also more expensive or don’t provide this quality in support and deliverability. There are companies that charge a flat rate per successful SMS - 10 cents. If your business prospers you will rather pay 15$ a month rather than a high fare on SMS.
  3. In our subscription you also have 3 other verification methods available with Missed Call & Caller ID being free. We encourage our customers to use all of them in a complete flow to decrease cost and verify the maximum number of users.
What payment methods do you support?
We currently support credit card payments and prepaid wire transfer.
What does it mean you charge only for successful SMS?
A successful message is an SMS that CheckMobi successfully delivers across to our carrier and they mange to send it forward. It can be a case where a message is successful in sending but undelivered because phone is closed or destination number is unreachable. If SMS is failing because of CheckMobi then you won’t get charged.
